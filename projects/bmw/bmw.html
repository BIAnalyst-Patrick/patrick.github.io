Executive Summary

Problem Statement

Analyze BMW's financial performance across profitability, risk, leverage, and growth efficiency from 2017 to 2024 to understand impact on overall performance.

Action Taken

Performed in-depth analysis through visualizations, KPIs, and ratios derived from financial data to assess key performance areas.

Result

Strong recovery in profitability and growth despite fluctuations, with 59.09% increase in net income and 7.94% drop in revenue.

Core Issue

BMW needed to assess how effectively it is utilizing its assets and managing debt risk to sustain profitability and growth.

Key Questions

  • How efficiently is BMW turning revenue into profit?
  • How does debt risk influence profitability and asset turnover?
  • What trends can be observed in asset utilization and efficiency?
