1. Test Overview

Objective

1.1 Objective

Evaluate whether a new Call-to-Action (CTA) button design improves user engagement compared to the current design.

1.2 Hypothesis

Null Hypothesis (H₀)

There is no difference in click-through rates between the current and new CTA button designs

Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)

The new CTA button design has a different click-through rate than the current design

1.3 Success Metrics

Primary Metric

7.2 Implementation Plan

1
Phase 1: Immediate Rollout (Week 1)
  • Deploy new CTA button to 100% of users

Conclusion

The A/B test results provide compelling evidence that the new CTA button design significantly outperforms the original, delivering a remarkable 207% increase in click-through rate with extremely high statistical confidence. The improvement is both statistically and practically significant, with minimal risk and substantial business impact projected. Based on these findings, immediate implementation of the new CTA is strongly recommended. Continued monitoring and iterative optimization will ensure sustained performance gains and maximize value for the business.
