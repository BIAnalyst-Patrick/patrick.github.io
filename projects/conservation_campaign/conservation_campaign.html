Study Design & Methodology

Research Questions

Primary:

Does personalized messaging significantly outperform generic messaging for conservation campaign engagement?

Secondary:

What are the optimal timing and frequency parameters for maximum campaign effectiveness?

Experimental Design

  • Study Type: Randomized controlled trial with statistical validation
  • Sample Size: 588,101 unique users (sufficient for 99% power)
  • Randomization: Users allocated to treatment groups based on subscription preferences
